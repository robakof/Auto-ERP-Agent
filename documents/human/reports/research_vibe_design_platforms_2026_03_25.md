## 5. Market Trends (2025-2026)

1. **Multi-model is the new normal.** Competitive image generation models tripled since early 2024. No single model excels at everything — Flux 2 leads photorealism, ChatGPT dominates complex scenes, SeeDream optimizes speed, Nano Banana Pro has artistic character.

2. **Aggregation vs. depth tradeoff.** Multi-model platforms offer breadth but new model versions appear first on original developer's platform. Delay in integration.

3. **"Vibe design" as a concept** parallels "vibe coding" (coined by Karpathy, 2025). Describe the vibe in natural language, AI produces it. Moving from prompting individual models to orchestrating multiple models for consistent output.

4. **Product photos shift from clarity to emotion** — warmth, confidence, desire, aspiration, belonging. Emotion drives conversion.

5. **Enterprise challenge:** Speed and output solved; making output consistent, on-brand, and effective is the real frontier. Custom AI models trained on brand assets are emerging as the solution (Superside, Flair.ai).

6. **AI product photography projected impact:** Brands embraced AI for content production in 2025; 2026 is the year AI shapes the entire workflow — how visuals are made, tested, scaled, and personalized.
